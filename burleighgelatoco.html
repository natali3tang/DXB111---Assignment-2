Burleigh Gelato Co.

Because of its beautiful location on the Gold Coast, 4min walk away from the golden sands of Burleigh Beach, this delightful gelato shop provides a welcome relief from the sun.
Burleigh Gelato Co. provides a new sensation of taste, ranging from basic flavours like mint chop chip and strawberry sorbet to interesting flavours inspired by the coastal culture. This little gelato shop is worth it for its price, whether you're strolling down the street or want a sweet treat after hanging out with friends at the beach.
Immerse yourself in the tastes of Burleigh and discover why Burleigh Gelato Co. is a popular among locals and young people.
