Oh My, Waffle

Oh My, Waffle is a delicious dessert heaven on Burleigh that brings everybody with its large variety of choices. Oh My, Waffle is a dessert lover's paradise, featuring everything from classic faves like the Belgian waffle wrapped in rich, velvety chocolate to Bounty Bar flavoured icecream. With the super friendly staff, it's the ideal place to satisfy your sweet cravings and create lovely memories with your friends and family.
This lively waffle house is a lot more than just a dessert spot; it's a vibrant hangout where the fun never stops. Oh My, Waffle presents an exciting menu of waffle creations that not only taste amazing but are also Instagram-worthy masterpieces.
The comfortable atmosphere is completed with cozy seating and neon accents, sets the stage for fun gatherings with friends, making it the perfect spot for an after-school treat. Embrace the mouthwatering flavors and vibrant vibes of Oh My, Waffle during your Burleigh adventure, and discover why it's a go-to spot for teens.
