About us

Welcome to Burleigh Gems, this website was created 4 you! Burleigh Gems was born out of a passion for exploration and a sweet tooth that knows no bounds. We understand that you are always on the lookout for fun and unique experiences, and that's exactly what we aim to provide.


Our mission is simple - to uncover and share the hidden gems of Burleigh with the next generation of adventure-seekers. We're here to guide you through the world of yummy desserts and the hidden op shops with cute vintage clothes!


Dessert Delights

Our website is a treasure map for all dessert lovers. Whether you're into mouthwatering gelato, cakes, or the perfect waffles, we've got you covered. We'll show you where to find the sweetest treats and best bites!


Op Shop Adventures

But that's not all. Burleigh Gems is also your gateway to the world of op shops. From vintage fashion to one-of-a-kind collectables, our guide will lead you to the most exciting hidden gems in the op shopping scene. Explore, find your next going out fit for cheap.
